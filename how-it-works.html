Behind the Scenes: What Cory Does

Lightning-Fast Detection

The moment a new inquiry hits your CRM, Cory springs into action. Our system monitors for new leads 24/7 and triggers outreach within seconds.

  • Real-time CRM monitoring
  • Intelligent lead scoring
  • Priority queue management
  • Time zone optimization

Natural AI Conversation

Cory engages prospects with warm, natural conversation. She introduces herself, asks qualifying questions, and provides helpful information about your programs.

  • Human-like conversation flow
  • Context-aware responses
  • Voicemail detection & personalized messages
  • Multi-language support

Intelligent FAQ Responses

Trained on your specific programs, costs, and requirements, Cory provides accurate answers to common questions while gathering qualification data.

  • Custom program knowledge base
  • Admission requirements expertise
  • Financial aid information
  • Escalation to humans when needed

Smart Appointment Booking

For qualified prospects, Cory seamlessly books appointments directly into your counselors' calendars, handling scheduling conflicts and preferences automatically.

  • Real-time calendar integration
  • Counselor availability checking
  • Automatic confirmations & reminders
  • Buffer time insertion

Multi-Channel Follow-Up

If the initial call doesn't connect, Cory automatically follows up via SMS and email with personalized messages and calendar links.

  • Coordinated SMS & email sequences
  • Personalized messaging
  • Optimal timing algorithms
  • Opt-out compliance

Seamless Human Handoff

When prospects are ready to talk to a human, Cory provides your counselors with complete context, qualification notes, and next steps.

  • Complete conversation history
  • Lead scoring and qualification data
  • Recommended talking points
  • CRM integration and logging

Frequently Asked Questions
